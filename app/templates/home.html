This model was trained to predict only one of the two categories, politician or comedian. If you upload a photo that is neither, the model will still try to make the best guess, but it would be nonsense!

Built using fast.ai, trained on ~200 google images of past and current presidential candidates and comedians. I like checking out the DC standup comedy scene, and the numerous Dem presidential debates got me thinking: what is the difference between a politican and a comedian as an orator visually? I'm training another model with the same photos where the face is blocked in order to prevent the model from predicting based on face recognition.
